FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your portfolio. Still have questions? WhatsApp us!

How long does it take to complete my portfolio?
Most portfolios are delivered within 24–48 hours of receiving your content (details, projects, photo). For the Elite plan, we offer a priority 12-hour turnaround. We'll confirm the exact timeline when you place your order.
What information do I need to provide?
You'll need to share: your name and contact details, a short bio/about me, your skills and technologies, 3–5 project descriptions (with screenshots if available), your education details, and a profile photo. We'll send you a simple form via WhatsApp to fill out.
Will I get the source code?
Yes! All plans include full source code (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). You get 100% ownership of the code — no subscriptions, no licensing fees. You can host it anywhere and modify it freely.
Can I request revisions after delivery?
Absolutely. We offer free revisions within 7 days of delivery for all plans. The Elite plan includes 1 month of free revisions. We want you to be 100% happy with the result.
How do I pay and how does the process work?
The process is simple: (1) Contact us via WhatsApp or the form below. (2) Share your details using our form. (3) Make payment via UPI / GPay / PhonePe. (4) Receive your portfolio within the promised timeframe. We accept all major UPI platforms.
